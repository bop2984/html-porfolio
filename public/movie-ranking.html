The Best Movies According to Bach

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spirited Away

This is my favourite movie of all time. The animation is breathtaking, and the story is both whimsical and profound. Hayao Miyazaki's work is a true masterpiece that resonates with audiences of all ages.

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a powerful story of hope and friendship. The performances by Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman are unforgettable. A must-see for any movie lover.

Drive

Super beutiful movie with a great story and characters. The animation is stunning, and the music is hauntingly beautiful. A true masterpiece.
